About Achievers page

The achievers Page of RCS helps students and staffs to showcase their special skills and talents to the whole Department.

The Page is divided into two sections:

  • Academic Achievers
  • Co-Curricular Achievers

Academic Achievers

This section contains the list of achievers in academic field.

Co-Curricular Achievers

This section contains the list of achievers in non-academic field. i.e) Other than academic related stuffs.

How to get placed in achievers page ?

You can be get placed in the Achievers Page, If

  • You are the Topper of the Anna University results at the end of all semesters
  • You have created any innovative things
  • you have special skills that other students/staffs doesn't
  • You have done any projects that is useful for the society

If you met up with any one of the above requirements, then you can discuss with your class incharge or directly with me through the "Suggestions" tab in settings.

After verification you wil be placed in the achievements Page of RCS.

For more information, contact your class incharge or use the Suggestions tab in Settings
