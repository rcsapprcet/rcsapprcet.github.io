What is RCS?

RCS is an android application which is developed specially for the Department of Computer Science and engineering of RCET

What can I do with RCS ?

You can do the following things with RCS :

  • View Anna University Results, Internal Assessment Marks, etc.
  • View your Internal Assessment Marks which is directly uploaded by your staffs.
  • Submit Leave Reports instantly.
  • Get regular notifications from the Department of any circulars, updates, etc.
  • Don't miss any events. Get information about the upcoming events (Inter-college and Intra-College Events).
  • Access your Linways account within a click.
  • Staff Feedback gets easier with RCS
  • Access to larger resources of Study Materials for all the semesters.

We have Whatsapp groups, then why RCS ?

Yeah, you have Whatsapp groups. But that thing is suitable for single class students. But if we need to connect the whole department, then Whatsapp is not fair. Right?

So to overcome this, RCS has been developed. Here the whole Department can be connected securely without sharing their personal information.
