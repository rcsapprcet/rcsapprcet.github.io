Resources

Some resources ( vectors, images, svg, etc. ) used inside the app are downloaded from :

  • Freepik
    • Freepik.com is the #1 resources site for vector graphics for graphic designers.
    • Freepik helps you to find free vector art, illustrations, icons, PSD, and more.
  • Flaticon
    • Flaticon is the largest search engine of free icons in the world.
    • It offers users, high quality graphic designs which is totally editable.
